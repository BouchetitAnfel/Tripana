Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan later?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any differences in pricing.
Is there a free trial?
Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for the Adventurer and Connoisseur plans. No credit card required to start your trial.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans.
Do you offer student discounts?
Yes! Students receive an additional 15% off all paid plans. Just verify your student status with a valid student ID or email.
What's your refund policy?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all plans. If you're not satisfied, contact us for a full refund within the first 30 days.
Can I share my account with others?
The Adventurer plan allows up to 3 users, while the Connoisseur plan allows up to 5 users. Perfect for families or travel groups!
